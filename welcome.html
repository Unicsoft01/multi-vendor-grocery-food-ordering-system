1.1       Background of the Study

The proposed system, "AreaFood," emerges against the backdrop of the increasing reliance on technology in various aspects of daily life, including food ordering and delivery services. With the proliferation of smartphones and the widespread availability of mobile applications, consumers have come to expect convenient and efficient ways to order food from their favorite restaurants. However, in many areas, traditional manual systems for food ordering and delivery remain prevalent, presenting challenges such as limited accessibility, inefficiency, and lack of real-time information. In response to these challenges, the "AreaFood" system seeks to modernize and digitize the food ordering process, leveraging mobile technology and location-based services to enhance the overall customer experience and streamline operations for restaurants amartha, (2020).

The development of "AreaFood" is also influenced by broader trends in the food delivery industry, including the rise of online food delivery platforms and the growing demand for convenience-driven services. As consumers increasingly turn to digital channels for their dining needs, there is a growing opportunity for innovative solutions that bridge the gap between restaurants and customers, offering seamless ordering experiences and personalized services. By harnessing the power of mobile technology and location-based services, "AreaFood" aims to meet the evolving needs and expectations of modern consumers while providing restaurants with tools to optimize their operations and reach a wider audience urama, (2021).

Furthermore, the proposed system is situated within the context of Anyigba and its unique characteristics as a community with its own set of challenges and opportunities in the food delivery space. By conducting a case study focusing on Anyigba restaurants, the research behind "AreaFood" seeks to understand the specific needs, preferences, and constraints of local residents and businesses Uzairu, (2010). This localized approach ensures that the "AreaFood" system is tailored to the context of Anyigba, taking into account factors such as local cuisines, cultural preferences, infrastructure limitations, and market dynamics. In doing so, "AreaFood" aims to not only improve the efficiency and convenience of food ordering in Anyigba but also contribute to the overall development and modernization of the local food delivery ecosystem.
