The proposed system, "AreaFood," presents a significant improvement over the existing manual system of food ordering by introducing a digital platform that streamlines the ordering process, enhances convenience for customers, and improves operational efficiency for restaurants. By leveraging mobile technology and location-based services, the "AreaFood" application offers users in Anyigba a user-friendly interface for browsing menus, placing orders, and tracking deliveries directly from their smartphones. This digital solution eliminates the need for manual order taking and processing, reducing errors, and delays while providing real-time information about menu availability, pricing, and order status.

The "AreaFood" system introduces features such as real-time order tracking, online payment options, and personalized recommendations, which enhance the overall customer experience and satisfaction. Customers can track the progress of their orders in real-time, receive notifications about order status updates, and make secure payments electronically, eliminating the need for cash transactions and improving transaction security. Additionally, the system's integration with Google Maps and Google Location API enables users to discover nearby restaurants based on their current location, expanding their choice and accessibility of food options in Anyigba.
